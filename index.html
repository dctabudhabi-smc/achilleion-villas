Gastouri · Corfu

Where the Empress chose to retreat.

For a Greek island, Corfu has an extraordinary number of inland villages - but none as grand, or as famous, as Gastouri. Wealthy families built their homes here for its water, its strategic position and its diverse green long before the Achilleion Palace rose. Over a million visitors see Empress Elisabeth's palace each year. Few ever discover why she chose this place.
